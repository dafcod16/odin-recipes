Authentic Fried Rice

Description:

Fried rice is a quick and easy meal that allows you to improvise and substitute many ingredients depending on what you have at home.
My favorite thing about this dish is that is incredibly cheap to make and you probably have most of these ingredients lying around your pantry already.

Ingredients

  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Veggies (Carrots, onions, green onions, and peas)
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Toasted Seasame Oil
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Scramble your eggs: Using a small pat of butter, scramble some eggs in a large
    sauté pan, breaking them into small pieces as you go. Then transfer the eggs to a
    separate plate, and set aside.
  2. Saute your veggies and garlic: Return your sauté pan to the heat, and sauté the
    onions, carrots, peas and garlic until soft and cooked through.
  3. Stir fry the rice: Then turn the burner to high heat. Scooch the veggies over to one
    side of the pan, melt the remaining butter in the other half, and add the chilled
    rice, soy sauce, and oyster sauce (if using). Then stir to combine with the veggies
    and continue sautéing the rice, stirring every 15-20 seconds or so for 3 minutes,
    or until you notice the rice and veggies starting to brown slightly.
  4. Temove pan from heat: And stir in your green onions, sesame oil, and scrambled
    eggs.
  5. Taste and season. Give the rice a taste, and season with salt and pepper, plus any
    extra soy sauce or sesame oil if needed.
  6. Serve! Then dish it up and serve nice and warm!
